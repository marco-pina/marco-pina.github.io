Marco Piña, PhD student in Economics

I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Virginia, focusing on the intersection of macrofinance and computational methods. My research interests include developing and computing partial equilibrium and DSGE models to study the dynamics of economic systems, as well as modeling econometric models of financial time series for portfolio and risk management.

Welcome to my personal website. Here you will find information about my research projects and also some codes I have made available on Github. If you are interested in discussing potential research projects or exchanging ideas on macrofinance or financial econometrics, please feel free to connect with me.
