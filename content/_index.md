Marco Piña, PhD student in Economics

I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Virginia, specializing in the intersection of macrofinance and computational methods. My research focuses on developing and computing partial equilibrium and DSGE models to analyze macroeconomic policy and financial markets . I also model econometric financial time series for forecasting and for portfolio and risk management.

Welcome to my personal website. Here you will find information about my research projects and also some codes I have made available on Github. If you are interested in discussing potential research projects or exchanging ideas on macrofinance or financial econometrics, please feel free to connect with me.
